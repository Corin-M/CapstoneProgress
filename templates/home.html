What is a Social Story?

Social stories/narratives are traditionally written and illustrated first- or third-person stories that inform the reader of the future events, expectations, rules, and potential triggers of the experience described by the story. About Our Collection:

This is the landing site for Corin Magee's senior capstone for Minerva University 2022-2023. The purpose of this project is to compile a consolidated resource for learning about social stories, accessing pre-made social stories in one library, and a guide to creating your own social stories.
